Workflow

From Upload to Approval in Minutes

A streamlined journey for members and claims staff.

1

Member Uploads a Claim

Through the IHP Mobile App or Employee Portal. Snaps a photo of a receipt, picks from gallery, or uploads a PDF. English or Chinese — both work seamlessly.

2

AI Reads & Extracts in Seconds

Our AI engine reads the document — whether it's a digital PDF or a photo of a crumpled receipt — and extracts every relevant field: date, merchant, amount, line items, diagnosis, policy number, and more. Each field gets a confidence score.

3

Member Reviews & Corrects

High-confidence fields are auto-accepted. Low-confidence fields are highlighted for review — the member can edit before submitting. If the receipt is older than 3 months, the app warns them. The system tracks what was changed.

4

Claims Team Reviews & Approves

The claim appears on the Admin Portal in real-time. Reviewers see the AI-extracted data, the original document, what the member changed, and an AI assessment. They approve, reject, or add notes — all in one place.
