The API

Simple to Integrate. Powerful Under the Hood.

A single REST endpoint. Standard HTTP. JSON response. Your team can integrate it in a day.

API Request 
POST /api/v1/extract

Headers:
  X-API-Key: your-api-key
  Content-Type: multipart/form-data

Body:
  file: receipt.pdf
Response (JSON) 
{
  "merchant": {
    "value": "ABC Medical Centre",
    "confidence": 9.2
  },

    "value": 328.50,
    "confidence": 9.8

Workflow

How It Fits Into Your Flow

Your systems stay the same. SkubbsVision slots in as the intelligence layer.

1

User Uploads a Document

Through your mobile app, employee portal, or claims admin — wherever your users already submit claims. Nothing changes on the front-end.

2

Your System Calls Our API

One POST /api/v1/extract call with the document attached. That's it — a single HTTP request from your backend.

3

SkubbsVision Extracts & Scores

Our AI engine reads the document — image or PDF, English or Chinese — and returns structured data with confidence scores for every field. Under 3 seconds.

4

Your App Uses the Data

Auto-populate forms, flag uncertain fields for user review, route to admin for approval — your rules, your UX. We just provide the intelligence.

Quality Assurance

Built-In Confidence Scoring

Every field comes with a confidence score. Your application decides the thresholds — auto-accept high confidence, flag low confidence for review.

8 - 10
High Confidence

Clear, unambiguous data. Your app can auto-accept these fields without user intervention.

5 - 7
Medium Confidence

Some ambiguity. Your app can prompt users to verify before proceeding.

0 - 4
Low Confidence

Significant uncertainty. Your app can highlight these for manual entry.

You define the thresholds. Our API gives you the data and the confidence — your business logic decides what happens next.
